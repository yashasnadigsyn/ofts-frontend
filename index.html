About OFTS

OFTS (Open Fotos Tagging System) is a powerful, open-source photo management platform designed to help you organize and rediscover your memories. With OFTS, you can effortlessly search, and manage your photo collection, all while maintaining complete control over your data. Our system leverages advanced face recognition and image captioning technologies to provide a seamless and intuitive experience, even on systems with limited resources.

Why OFTS?

In a world where privacy and control are paramount, OFTS stands out as a self-hosted, open-source alternative to proprietary photo management systems. Whether you're a tech enthusiast or someone who simply wants to organize their photos, OFTS offers a lightweight, efficient solution that respects your privacy and empowers you to manage your memories on your terms.
